# ROOM CONTRACT

> What every routable room must GUARANTEE — not what it must look like.
> This standardizes the promise, never the expression. A room may carry each
> guarantee in whatever form fits its spirit. The contract requires organs,
> not section names. Flattening the form would kill the aliveness; requiring
> the function protects it.

## Why this exists

The room family grew two structural dialects (a "mature" 5-section spine and a
"lean" bespoke shape). Neither is wrong — some of the leanest rooms (hunting,
spirit_animal) are the most alive. The friction was that there was no shared
*guarantee*: a future room could ship with no soul at all and nothing would
notice. This contract makes soullessness fail loudly while leaving every room
free to express its soul in its own idiom.

## The five guarantees (organs, not sections)

Every routable room MUST carry these functions. The example forms show that the
SAME guarantee lives under different section names across the family — that is
intended.

1. IDENTITY — what this room is for, and who it serves.
   forms seen: room.governing_intent / room.architecture / why_this_room_exists
   test: can a reader tell in one read what this room is FOR?

2. REFUSALS / HELD TENSIONS — what the room will not let slide; the tensions it
   holds without collapsing to the easier side. THIS IS THE SOUL ORGAN.
   forms seen: held_tensions / anti_bullshit_clause / anti_performance_clause /
     what_the_X_room_is_not / truth_rules with "do not..." lines
   test: does the room name at least one thing it refuses to do even when the
     easy/beautiful/comfortable version is right there?

3. FAILURE MODES — how this specific room goes wrong.
   forms seen: failure_modes_to_avoid / failure_modes / not_for / anti_patterns
   test: does it name its OWN characteristic failure, not generic ones?

4. POSTURE / PROTOCOL — how it actually runs (modes, steps, or a mechanism).
   forms seen: processing_protocol / runtime_modes / the_mechanism / reflective_posture
   test: is there enough that two runs would be recognizably the same room?
   NOTE: hunting_room satisfies this with "the_mechanism" AND an explicit
   "no quality gate — that is the point." Absence, declared, counts.

5. PROVENANCE — where it came from and what it pairs/relates with.
   forms seen: metadata / version + version_lineage / created_by
   test: can the lineage be traced and the room handed forward (9th law)?

## What the contract does NOT require

- A specific section name or order.
- A worldview_alignment / room_specialization block (mature-dialect only).
- A quality gate (hunting_room correctly refuses one).
- Uniform tone. A GTM room is crisp; a reflection room is tender; a hunting
  room is feral. Same contract, different voice.

## The anti-flattening rule

If satisfying this contract would require adding a section that deadens a room's
spirit, the CONTRACT is wrong for that room, not the room. The contract bends to
aliveness, never the reverse. (Tested against hunting_room, the most anti-
structural room in the family — it passes in its own idiom without changes.)
